General Practitioner - Full-time

Location: London, UK

Job Description

We are looking for a compassionate and dedicated General Practitioner (GP) to join our healthcare team. As a GP at Docplanner, you will provide comprehensive medical care, diagnose a wide range of health conditions, and ensure patients receive the best possible treatment. This is a full-time position based in London.

Responsibilities

  • Diagnosing and treating a wide range of medical conditions.
  • Providing preventive care and health education to patients.
  • Collaborating with specialists and other healthcare providers for complex cases.
  • Maintaining accurate patient records and providing referrals when necessary.

Qualifications

  • Medical degree and board certification in General Medicine.
  • At least 3 years of experience working as a General Practitioner.
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills.
  • Ability to work both independently and in a collaborative team environment.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Flexible working hours.
  • Access to state-of-the-art medical facilities.
  • Professional development opportunities and support.
